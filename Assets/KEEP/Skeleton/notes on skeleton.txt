notes on skeleton:

Skeleton can carry boxes, spears, swords, shields, throwing items.

Skeleton cannot carry guns or rifles or items that fire projectiles.

white skeleton has low health and can't see ledges.

red skeleton had higher health, can throw bones and can see ledges.

skeleton can pickup shield if empty, which auto repels frontal attacks for the life of the shield (pickup shield, 
two hand and one hand items is crouch)
